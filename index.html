2 BHK Interior Design Cost in Bangalore (2026 Guide)

Bangalore homeowners are increasingly investing in smart, space-saving interiors. If you're planning a 2 BHK interior design in Bangalore, understanding the latest pricing, trends, and design strategies can help you save money and create a stylish home.

Average 2 BHK Interior Cost in Bangalore (2026)

In 2026, interior design costs in Bangalore depend on materials, customization, and design complexity.

  • Basic Interiors: ₹3.5L – ₹5L (Rental or budget homes)
  • Mid-Range Interiors: ₹5L – ₹8L (Most popular choice)
  • Premium Interiors: ₹8L – ₹15L+ (Luxury homes)

Room-Wise Interior Cost Breakdown

Living Room Interior Cost

  • TV Unit: ₹25K – ₹1L
  • Sofa & Furniture: ₹40K – ₹2L
  • False Ceiling & Lighting: ₹30K – ₹1.2L

Modular Kitchen Cost in Bangalore

  • Modular Kitchen Setup: ₹1.5L – ₹4L
  • Chimney & Hob: ₹15K – ₹60K

Bedroom Interior Cost

  • Wardrobes: ₹80K – ₹2.5L
  • Bed with Storage: ₹40K – ₹1.5L

Top Interior Design Trends in Bangalore (2026)

Interior design trends in Bangalore are shifting towards minimalism, functionality, and smart living.

  • Minimalist modular interiors with clean finishes
  • Smart storage solutions for small apartments
  • Neutral color palettes (white, beige, grey)
  • Matte finish laminates replacing glossy surfaces
  • Smart lighting and automation systems

Modular vs Custom Interiors

  • Modular Interiors: Affordable, fast installation, modern look
  • Custom Interiors: Expensive but highly personalized

How to Reduce Interior Design Cost

  • Choose laminate instead of veneer
  • Limit false ceiling designs
  • Use ready-made furniture
  • Plan storage efficiently
  • Compare multiple interior designers

Why Interior Design is Worth Investment in Bangalore

With rising real estate prices in Bangalore, interior design helps maximize space utilization and increases property value. A well-designed 2 BHK can significantly improve comfort and resale value.
